Oatmeal Recipe Written By Juliet Musoke

Ingredients

  • 2 package of oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • saucepan

Directions

  1. Get meadium saucepan
  2. Pour milk in the oatmeal packages
  3. Use meadium temperture and cook for 3 minuets
  4. Turn off stove and prepare to serve
